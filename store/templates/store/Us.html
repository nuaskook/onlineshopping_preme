    Our Product Types

    There are 2 types of products on our website:

    1.Pre-ordered products 2.Products in stock

    Which the web will already notify the type of product on the

    information page of that product, before adding to cart.

    What is Pre-order?

    Pre-order is to order products in advance. Which the ordered products will not be received immediately. As some products will take time to be purchased from abroad, the delivery time also depends on the transportation.

    Payment Method

    There is only 1 round for product payment.

    The buyer has to pay for the product in full price first, before the website can proceed to the next step.

    How Long Does Pre-order Usually Take?

    Normally, products ordered from abroad will take different import

    times depending on the shipping method of that particular product. Therefore the pre-order takes an average of 15-30 days from the order to the point that the buyer has received the product.
